    Remediation programs and system change often run into the same issues that created the original risk in the first place: unclear decisions, weak governance, poor coordination, and too much pressure on the wrong people. The result is usually delay, rework, and unnecessary cost.

    We help organisations put stronger structure around high-risk payroll change before delivery begins and, where needed, provide independent governance and risk oversight throughout the program.
    
    __Best suited to organisations experiencing:__
    
    - payroll remediation or regulator scrutiny
    - a major payroll, HRIS or T&A system change
    - a prior failed implementation
    - concern that the business is not ready for complex payroll change
    - a need for stronger governance during execution
    <br/>
    __What this can include:__
    
    - remediation or change readiness assessment
    - governance and escalation design
    - decision-making guardrails
    - capacity and dependency mapping
    - go-live or program risk gates
    - independent governance and risk oversight during delivery
