    Many payroll functions are being held together by workarounds, goodwill, and institutional knowledge. That may keep things moving, but it does not create a payroll function that is stable, scalable, or easy to defend when issues arise.
    
    This sprint is designed to strengthen the structure around payroll, so the function is not being held together by manual effort, workarounds, and key-person reliance.
    
    __Best suited to organisations experiencing:__
    
    - repeated near misses or recurring payroll issues
    - unclear ownership or decision-making
    - too much reliance on key individuals
    - chronic payroll pressure or operational fragility
    - growth, complexity, or change that the current model cannot support
    
    __What this can include:__
    
    - target payroll operating model
    - role clarity and decision ownership
    - governance forums and escalation pathways
    - control accountability model
    - leadership reporting structure
    - risk-prioritised roadmap for implementation
