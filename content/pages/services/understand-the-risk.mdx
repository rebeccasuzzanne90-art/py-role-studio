  body: |
    Payroll risk rarely sits in one place. It usually shows up across unclear ownership, weak controls, broken handoffs between teams, poor visibility, and decisions being made without fully understanding the payroll impact. This review helps organisations identify where the exposure sits, what is contributing to it, and where to focus first.
    
    __Best suited to organisations experiencing:__
    - underpayment concerns or audit pressure
    - executive uncertainty about payroll exposure
    - recurring payroll issues with no clear root cause
    - payroll team stress, turnover, or burnout
    - a sense that risk is building, but no clear picture of where
    
    __What this can include:__
    
    - payroll risk and governance assessment
    - decision and dependency mapping
    - control effectiveness review
    - upstream process risk across HR, Finance and Operations
    - executive-ready summary of findings
    - practical priorities for the next 90 days
